About Me

Hey! I'm Sivasubramanian — an Electronics & Communication engineer with a deep passion for the world where software controls physical hardware. Whether it's a microcontroller running a sensor loop or a robot navigating on its own, I love the moment code becomes motion.

My focus is on low-level firmware, embedded C, and real-time systems. I've worked hands-on with 8051, AVR, and ARM microcontrollers, and I'm actively building skills in ROS and PCB design.

I believe good embedded engineering is about reliability under constraints — there's no room for waste when your resources are measured in kilobytes.
